PointsIQ Lean Canvas
Loyalty portfolio tracker and award optimizer

?Problem

List top 1 to 3 problems
  1. Scattered balances. Points sit across multiple programs; no clear view of total value.
  2. Points expire. Programs remove balances after periods of inactivity; users lose value without notice.
  3. Low value use. Users redeem at about zero point six cents per point while better options reach five to thirteen cents.

💡Solution

Outline a possible solution for each problem
  1. Portfolio dashboard. Secure OAuth connections to each program; balances shown in one place and valued in United States dollars.
  2. Expiration tracking. Ongoing monitoring with alerts issued weeks before points expire.
  3. Redemption optimizer. Ranked options by value per point; aligned with actual balances.

★Unique Value Proposition

Single, clear, compelling message that states why you are different and worth paying attention
Award Hacker meets Seat.aero for owned points. Full portfolio tracked, valued, optimized. System starts from inventory; it knows balances, value, expiry, and best available redemption.

♞Unfair Advantage

Something that cannot easily be bought or copied
  • Portfolio data moat. After six connected programs, switching costs rise; inventory layer remains distinct.
  • Proprietary valuation model. Real time cents per point from live availability.
  • Negotiated affiliate economics. Direct issuer relationships supported by conversion data.
  • Founder credibility in points and travel communities.
