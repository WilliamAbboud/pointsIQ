The problem

Your points are scattered, expiring, and quietly losing value.

The average traveler has points across five or more programs and never knows what they are worth, when they expire, or which redemption gives the best return. Billions of dollars in loyalty value go unused every year.

🧩
Scattered balances
Your miles and points live across a dozen apps and emails. Nobody has a single number for what it is all worth.
⏰
Silent expiration
Programs quietly wipe balances after 12 to 24 months of inactivity. You find out after the fact, if at all.
📉
Leaving value on the table
Using points for gift cards gets you 0.6¢ each. Transfer them right and the same points are worth 11¢ or more.
